What is
Pet Number Project?

Pet Number Project is a decentralized pet registry project, and it's primary objective is to make a happy society for pets and humans by establishing an efficient technological infrastructure and a broad connection of all animal related services.

Our platform will meet the legal rules within the pet registry system currently existing each state or country and aim to be the adoption of the decentralized pet registry as the legal ledger of record for pet ownership transfers in each country.

    The 3 key points we must accomplish:


    • Raise the sense of responsibility and awareness towards pets as lifetime partners.

    • Promoting animal adoption over purchasing.

    • Removal of inhumane pet businesses.

What can Pet Number do?

Our Decentrized Pet Registry System will enable transparent and secured information sharing between all pet owners and businesses involved. Thanks to Blockchain and Smart Comtract technology, Pet Number will bring light and eliminate illegal or inhumane activities in the pet industry, such as overbreeding at puppy mills and mistreating animals at pet stores.

Why Blockchain and Smart Contract?

Blockchain is still a slow database to use, but has proven to be one of the biggest inventions since internet itself. It is a database shared across the network of computers, enabling all involved entities to be visible from anyone. Blockchain has been making huge impacts on our daily lives, and now it's time to step forward and work together to create a better world for both humans and pets.

By combining Smart Contract, a computer protocol which digitally enforces the negotiation or performance of a contract, Pet Number can accomplish credible transaction and transparency in the pet industry.

What are the benefits of using Pet Number?

  • Transparency of animal distribution can be secured.
  • All companies and businesses involved will be background checked for legal, humane and/or licensed business activity.
  • Everyone can monitor and report abuse, irresponsible and suspicious business activities.
  • Persinalized support for users.
  • Eliminate paper works for spay/neuter agreement, breed papers, health insurance, return policy, and more.

Pet Number will create the new structure of pet distribution

Due to the current closed pet distribution channel, many bad businesses such as puppy mills are able to go on dispite their inhumane breeding practice. As stated, Blockchain can bring transparency to the pet industry solving potential problems caused by those bad businesses.
