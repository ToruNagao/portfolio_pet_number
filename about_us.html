Who We Are

Welcome to Pet Number!

We are an internet-based, decentralized pet registry system, designed to efficiently and securely manage and monitor pet ownership, and create a much better society for pets and humans alike.

We want to create a powerful, outstanding network between pet owners and businesses using blockchain technology, allowing us to connect people with the highest security and transparency. By ensuring all legal requirements are met when registering to our system, we can expose and eliminate any illegal or inhumane pet businesses. In short, Pet Number is a system that will help save animal lives and promote animal welfare.

In addition, we are working hard on a wonderful idea to build a community through an SNS platform, connecting volunteers, veterinarians, pet shops, and pet owners. This platform is designed to offer a professional value and power to improve the volunteer and animal welfare infrastructures.

The Pet Number concept was founded in San Francisco, but we will start the project in Japan where the technological and industrial environment is ideal, then expand globally. All the company’s efforts and numerous projects will be fully focused on raising awareness of pet wellness, promoting adoption, and removing inhumane businesses in the pet industry.

We are tremendously grateful to all the support of established animal welfare organizations and industry professionals, and welcome any large or small contribution.

If you feel the same as we do about eliminating pet abuse and want every pet to have a happy life, don’t hesitate to get in touch and support our project. Let’s help every pet find a happy life, together.
